Paget was born Paget Valerie Brewster on 10th of March, 199 in Concord, Massachusetts USA. Her father, Galen Brewster work as a school administrator while her mother, Hathaway Brewster work as a Government official, Paget have a younger brother name Ivan whom she describe as having a very good relationship with.

As a child, growing up she had interest in design and wanted to become a designer. After graduating from high school, she relocated to New York to attend College, she enroll in parsons school of design, a private art and design school. In her first year as a freshman she got interested in acting and began acting, she decided to drop out of design school to pursue a career in acting; it was a huge risk taking the decision to quit design knowing fully well she will make out quite a good career; also uncertain about the success of the acting career she’s venturing into. She moved to San Francisco, enrolled in Jean Shelton Studio and began attending acting class
