Paget made her debut in the famous television series “friend”, her role in the series really spur her to limelight as the series soon became famous. In the same year, she also starred in the movie let’s talk about sex playing the role as Michelle. She has appeared on numerous Tv series such as Ghost cop, Godzilla: the series, love and money and many more other series. Ms. Brewster had a long run with popular Tv series Criminal Minds playing the role as Emily Prentiss a multilingual Supervisory special agent, the show really got her so many fans who really love her for a role. She lasted till the sixth season where she was release from her contract alongside her co-start A.J Cook in what was a “Cost Cutting move” by CBS. It took the timely intervention and much protest from Paget’s fan for her to be recalled back into the show. In 2012, she announced she was leaving the show (Criminal Minds) at the end of the season to pursue a career in comedy but it seems her attachment to the show isn’t letting her go off completely as she keeps on coming back; in season nine of the show, she soon reprise her role for the shows 200th episode as a guest star.
